Coffee Shop Empire - Corporate Database
=======================================

Welcome to the Coffee Shop Empire data repository. This contains all operational data,
historical records, and planning documents for our global coffee chain.

Structure:
- /franchises/ - Location-specific data and operations
- /coffee_beans/ - Sourcing, tasting notes, and inventory
- /employees/ - Staff records, training, schedules
- /customers/ - Feedback, loyalty programs, demographics
- /operations/ - Daily operations, supply chain, logistics
- /marketing/ - Campaigns, menus, branding materials
- /finance/ - Revenue reports, budgets, projections

Last updated: 2024-11-15
