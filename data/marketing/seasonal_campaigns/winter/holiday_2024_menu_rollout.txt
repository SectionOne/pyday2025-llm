HOLIDAY 2024 MENU ROLLOUT STRATEGY
==================================
Campaign Name: "Cozy Winter Wonderland"
Launch Date: November 15, 2024
Duration: November 15 - January 5, 2025
Marketing Budget: $2.5 million
Target Revenue Increase: 35% vs previous year

NEW HOLIDAY BEVERAGES:

1. Peppermint Mocha Supreme
   - Description: Premium espresso, steamed milk, peppermint syrup, dark chocolate sauce, whipped cream, candy cane pieces
   - Price: $6.25 (Grande), $6.75 (Venti)
   - Target Market: Adults 25-45
   - Expected Sales: 8,500 units/day globally

2. Gingerbread Latte Deluxe
   - Description: Espresso with gingerbread syrup, steamed milk, topped with whipped cream and gingerbread cookie crumbs
   - Price: $5.95 (Grande), $6.45 (Venti)
   - Target Market: Families, holiday shoppers
   - Expected Sales: 6,200 units/day globally

3. Eggnog Spiced Chai
   - Description: Black tea, steamed eggnog, chai spices, vanilla syrup, dash of nutmeg
   - Price: $5.75 (Grande), $6.25 (Venti)
   - Target Market: Tea drinkers, traditional holiday fans
   - Expected Sales: 4,100 units/day globally

4. Winter Wonderland Hot Chocolate
   - Description: Premium Belgian chocolate, steamed milk, topped with marshmallows and edible glitter
   - Price: $5.25 (Grande), $5.75 (Venti)
   - Target Market: Children, families, non-coffee drinkers
   - Expected Sales: 7,800 units/day globally

5. Cranberry Orange Spice Tea
   - Description: Black tea with cranberry, orange peel, cinnamon, and cloves
   - Price: $4.25 (Grande), $4.75 (Venti)
   - Target Market: Health-conscious customers, tea enthusiasts
   - Expected Sales: 3,200 units/day globally

COMPETITIVE ANALYSIS:

Starbucks Holiday Menu:
- Similar drink offerings
- Earlier launch (November 1)
- Higher price points
- Stronger brand recognition

Dunkin' Holiday Menu:
- Focus on value pricing
- More traditional options
- Limited availability
- Regional variations

Local Coffee Shops:
- Unique local specialties
- Smaller marketing budgets
- Community-focused approach
- Higher ingredient quality
